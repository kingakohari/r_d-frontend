Accessibility Statement for Frontend Development

We at Frontend Development are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

We aim for our website to be in conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. While we strive to adhere to these guidelines, there may be some areas that are not fully compliant.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes features such as keyboard navigation, text alternatives for images, and high-contrast modes to enhance accessibility.

Known Limitations

We are aware that some areas of our website may not fully meet accessibility standards, including eye-tracking. We are working to address these issues.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. Please let us know if you encounter any barriers by contacting us at info@acmeinc.com or +36 123 456 789.

Assistance

If you need any assistance or require information in an alternative format, please contact us.
